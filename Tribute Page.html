Chhatrapathi Shivaji Maharaj, also spelled Sivaji (born February 19, 1630 , or April 1627, Shivneri Fort, Poona [now Pune], india-died April 3, 1680, Rajgarh), founder of the Maratha Kingdom of India. The Kingdom's security was based on religious taleration and on the functinal integration of the Brahmans, Marathas, and Prabhus.


At the time of his birth, the families of his father Shahaji and his mother jihabai both served the Ahmednagar Sultanate in miltary and administrative capacities, although they subsequently transferred their allegiance to the Mughal Empire and the Bijapur Suitanate at different times.


In the body, briefly present his achievements- he increasedthe influence of the Maratha Empire from Deccan to Karnataka and gave it a place at the all india level, he built an efficient administrative system, set up an authentic revenue base of the empire through Chauth along with army based on cash payment etc..

    Most of the earliest seurviving records of Shivaji's childhood were composed around 150 years after his birth. These records, especially the Marathi-language bakhar, contain several stories that are historical unreliable.
    Shivaji was born to Shahaji and Jihabai, at the time of the severe Mahadurga famine in the Maharashtra region. He had an elder brother named Sambhaji, and his parents had lost several othee children in infancy Shivaji Maharaj was born at the hill fort of Shivneri and his mothee named him after Shivai, a local goddess.
    Ancestry and self-identity
    During his coronation 1674, Shivai's royal priest Gaga Bhatta presented him as a member of the Kshatriya solar dynasty, counting the Sisodia Rajputs among his ancestors. The claim is also supported by Shiva-Bharata, a text composed ar Shivji's Court
    Historian Bai Krishna, relying on some firmans in possession of the chiefs of Mudhai (who claimed a share ancestry with Shivaji), traced the ancestry of Shivaji to the Sisodia chief Lakshmasimha, who died at the Siege of Chittargh against Alauddin Khaji.
    Conflicts over ancestry
    Shivaji was not a Kshatriya as required and hence had to coronation untill 1674 and hired Gaga Bhatt, a Brahmin scholor from Kashi to trace his ancestry back to the Sisodias. Historians such as Surendra Nath Sen and K.reject the Sisodia origin by citing the temple incription of Math.
    According to Sabhasad Bhaskhar, Shivaji doesn't belonged to Kshatriya varma. So, he had to win over to order to crown him at a the king.
